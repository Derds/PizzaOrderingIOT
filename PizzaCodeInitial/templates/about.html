About This Project

This website is part of my final year individual project for the BSC Computer Science in Cardiff University. Connected to a tangible object, this project aims to teach children of 9-11 years old about nutrition and the importance of making healthy choices.

Instructions for Use

  • First, connect your PizzaBox system to a power supply and go to the website address. [Often http://192.168.1.188:8080/]
  • Please double check the RFID reader has power
  • Next, once the power lights have turned on, add the toppings you want to your pizza.
  • Input your details and select order pizza.
  • You should now see some personalised nutrition data about your pizza
  • You can choose to make swaps of toppings, or remove a certain topping.
  • When you are happy you can click, select order.
  • Finally, you will have created your pizza.
  • Press "order again" to order another pizza.
