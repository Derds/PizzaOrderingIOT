Calorie Checker

Use this NHS tool to learn more about the calorie contents of different foods. The number of calories food has is to do with how much energy is in the food. Higher calories is a higher amount of energy, but eating too many calories in a day can mean you are taking in more energy than you use. Extra unused energy is converted to body fat.
